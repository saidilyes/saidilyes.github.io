About Me

I am currently teaching at Université de Technologie de Compiègne (UTC). After earning a master's degree in Transport, Mobility, and Network, I decided to pursue a Ph.D. in computer science. My primary passion lies in one hand in automation and robotics, but I am also deeply interested in mobility, particularly in optimizing energy consumption for hybrid and electric vehicles. On the other hand, I am very passionate to the different aspects of engineering.
Looking ahead, I am eager to join a team engaged in stimulating projects and creative ideas. I am excited about the opportunity to work on impactful projects that push the boundaries of what is possible in engineering.
